My academic interests

Hi, I'm Serhii! I have a bachelor's degree in International Relations from Andrzej Frycz Modrzewski Krakow University, Poland. I also have a master's degree in East European Studies from Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Ukraine. My current academic research delves into the dynamics of response to politically charged social media feeds during wartime, shedding light on the intersection of media consumption and political discourse. At UChicago, my focus is on Natural Language Processing (NLP) and AI, where I'm honing my skills in advanced text analysis and exploring the ethical and practical dimensions of artificial intelligence.
